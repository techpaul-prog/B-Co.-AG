Who we are

Built on precision.
Driven by results.

Founded in Zurich in 2008, B & Co. AG began as a boutique network consultancy and has since grown into a full-service IT partner for businesses across Switzerland, Germany, and Austria. Our clients range from family-run SMEs to publicly listed companies — all of whom rely on us to keep their technology running and evolving.

We are a team of 65 engineers, architects, consultants, and project managers. We work in tight delivery squads, staying hands-on throughout every engagement. No offshore handoffs, no one-size-fits-all solutions.

As a registered Aktiengesellschaft, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of accountability and transparency. Our business model is built on long-term partnerships — not short-term contracts.
